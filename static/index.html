How It Works

  1. What do you believe in? Whether it's cleaner air or food security for all, choose an organization that works towards your idea of a better world.
  2. Build your cheering squad. Invite friends to pledge a tiny incremental amount for every step you take or mile you run.
  3. Get off the couch! For every step you take or mile you run, you accrue micro-donations to fund your cause and help it grow.
